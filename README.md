# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior to identify purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and revenue trends. Using **Python**, **PostgreSQL**, and **Power BI**, the project transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights and an interactive dashboard for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Identify top-performing products and categories.
* Evaluate the impact of discounts and subscriptions on customer spending.
* Segment customers based on purchase history.
* Build an interactive Power BI dashboard to support business decisions.

---

* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn)
* **PostgreSQL**
* **Power BI**

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains approximately **3,900 customer purchase records** with the following information:

* Customer ID
* Age
* Gender
* Item Purchased
* Category
* Purchase Amount (USD)
* Location
* Review Rating
* Subscription Status
* Shipping Type
* Discount Applied
* Previous Purchases
* Payment Method
* Frequency of Purchases

## 🗄️ SQL Business Analysis

The following business questions were answered using PostgreSQL:

1. What is the total revenue generated by male and female customers?
2. Which customers used a discount but still spent more than the average purchase amount?
3. Which are the top 5 products with the highest average review rating?
4. Compare the average purchase amount between Standard and Express Shipping.
5. Do subscribed customers spend more than non-subscribers?
6. Which five products have the highest percentage of purchases with discounts applied?
7. Segment customers into New, Returning, and Loyal based on previous purchases.
8. What are the top three most purchased products within each category?
9. Are repeat buyers more likely to subscribe?
10. What is the revenue contribution of each age group?

## 💡 Key Insights

* Clothing generated the highest revenue among all product categories.
* Adult and middle-aged customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
* Subscribers spent more on average than non-subscribers.
* Highly rated products generally achieved stronger sales.
* Discount campaigns encouraged higher purchase activity.
* Repeat buyers showed a higher likelihood of being subscribed.

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

* Strengthen customer loyalty and subscription programs.
* Personalize promotions based on purchase history.
* Focus marketing on high-value customer segments.
* Promote highly rated products to increase sales.
* Optimize discount strategies using customer purchase behavior.
